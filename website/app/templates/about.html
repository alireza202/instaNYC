instaNYC tells you what is happening in New York City right now using Instagtam uploads. It compares every location to its own past, and determines if that location is seeing more/less uploads than usual. It is very good at finding events such as games, shows, concerts, clubs, and similar events, as they are happening!


You can also go back in time, and check what events were happening in the past. Make sure to check that out!


There are three types of markers on the map:

  • Calm: Less uploads than the 1st quartile of the hourly uploads of the last 5 days.
  • Busy: More uploads than the 3rd quartile of the hourly uploads of the last 5 days.
  • Event: Outlier! More uploads than the 1.5*IQR over the 3rd quartile of the hourly uploads of the last 5 days.
